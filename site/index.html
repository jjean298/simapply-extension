Features

FAQ

Good to know

Does SimApply preserve formatting perfectly?

Not yet. This is still an MVP, so layout, spacing, and styling can shift during editing and export.

Does AI rewrite my entire resume automatically?

It can suggest targeted rewrites and guided whole-resume drafts, but users should still review and refine the final version.

Will there be usage limits?

Yes. AI limits and a possible bring-your-own-key flow are part of the planned roadmap.
